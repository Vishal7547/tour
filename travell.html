Ghanta Ghar Clock Tower

Ghanta Ghar Clock Tower, Bhagalpur OverviewLocated in the center of the city, Ghanta Ghar Clock Tower is a famous tourist attraction in Bhagalpur. It was constructed in the bygone times to know the time. Today, the area around the place is bustling with shops and vehicles that zoom past it.Why Is It Famous?Ghanta Ghar Clock Tower is a tourist site in Bhagalpur. Due to its location in the center of Bhagalpur, it always remains crowded with locals and visitors. There are different shops and eateries located around that are frequently visited by people on a huge scale. At night, the clock tower illuminates due to lighting.Best Time To Visit:-There is no specific season for visiting the place since it stands in the center of the city. People can visit this place on a daily basis.Nearest Railway Station - Bhagalpur Junction Railway StationNearest Airport - Bhagalpur Airport
