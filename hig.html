TNB COLLEGE

Established in 1883, T.N.B College is a constituent college of T.M. Bhagalpur University. The college is a very prominent institution and is located in Bhagalpur which joins two important cities, Patna and Kolkata. At present the college offers undergraduate courses in almost all fields of Science and Arts. In addition, it also offers PG and research level courses in some disciplines. The college has been accredited grade ‘A’ by NAAC.
